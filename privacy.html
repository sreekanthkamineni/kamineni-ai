Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 30, 2026

1. Overview

Kamineni Solutions Inc. ("we," "our," or "us") provides enterprise AI-driven video rendering pipelines and infrastructure services. We value the privacy of our partners, business inquiries, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, process, and protect your personal information, in adherence to global data privacy laws.

2. B2B Contact Data Collection & Consent

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily submit to us via our business inquiry forms, specifically:

  • Name
  • Corporate Email Address
  • Inquiry Details and Project Message

Submission of this form requires explicit age verification confirming you are at least 18 years old. We collect this data solely for B2B lead generation, processing inquiries, and discussing project integrations. If you wish to withdraw consent, request data removal, or object to processing, contact us at founders@kamineni-ai.com.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our website and services are intended exclusively for commercial entities and individuals aged 18 or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Note on our Rendering Pipelines: While our AI video rendering technologies are optimized to produce content appropriate for children's media platforms, our client systems are commercial API layers. Any data ingestion workflows or user inputs are strictly audited under children's safety parameters to prevent collection, retention, or tracking of any children's personally identifiable information (PII). We comply with the United States Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) guidelines and do not track individual children.

4. Canadian Privacy Laws (PIPEDA & Ontario CPA)

We comply with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and Ontario consumer protection frameworks. We adhere to the ten fair information principles:

  • Accountability: We are responsible for the protection of personal data in our custody.
  • Identifying Purposes: The purpose for which information is collected is documented before collection (B2B business communications).
  • Consent: Explicit user consent is requested at the point of data entry.
  • Limiting Collection: We limit data collection to the minimum required for the inquiry.
  • Accuracy & Safeguards: Personal data is kept accurate, secure, and encrypted in transit.

5. General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Compliance

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation, individuals located in the European Economic Area (EEA) have specific data protection rights, including:

  • Right of Access: Request copies of the personal data we hold.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your data.
  • Right to Portability: Request transfer of your data to another organization.

To exercise these rights, please email us. We will respond within the statutory timeframe (normally 30 days).

6. Data Retention & Security

We employ industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect database records from unauthorized access. Personal data collected via forms is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the business relationship or as required by law. We do not sell, rent, or distribute personal information to third parties.
